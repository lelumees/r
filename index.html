Software Engineer / Designer / Kayaking enthusiast

If I had to describe myself in three five words.

Hey there. My name is Rasmus Lelumees and I have worked as a software engineer for over 7 years. This includes me setting up cloud infra for full-stack apps in startups like Testlio and writing server-side code for the Estonian government ministries. In the recent years I’ve been building Bolt Food mobile apps from ground up and leading a team of talented engineers at Bolt to support revenue growth from 0 to billions. Aside from that, I’ve done some pet projects like the most recent MVP Trello-meets-Kanban webapp.

Diving into technical details is great, but sometimes I desire having a direct impact on how things will be seen and felt by users. This is why I studied Interaction Design in the past and keep taking on some freelance work as a UI/UX designer.

When I’m not busy coding, designing, reading or browsing Product Hunt, I am likely in my kayak, paddling on nearby lakes and rivers.

P.S. I live in Estonia, a tiny Northeast-European country which is known for its vibrant tech startup scenery and can boast with various digital services, including e-Residency.
